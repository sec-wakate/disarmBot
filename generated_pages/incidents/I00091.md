# Incident I00091: Facebook uncovers Chinese network behind fake expert

* **Summary:** <i>“Facebook owner Meta Platforms has removed more than 500 accounts linked to an online disinformation network primarily based in China.<br><br> “The accounts had promoted the claims of a fake Swiss biologist called "Wilson Edwards", who alleged the US was meddling in efforts to find the origins of Covid-19.<br><br> “Edwards' comments had been widely carried by Chinese state media outlets.<br><br> “However, the Swiss embassy said that it was unlikely this person existed.<br><br> “Meta said in its report the social media campaign was "largely unsuccessful," and targeted English-speaking audiences in the United States and Britain and Chinese-speaking audiences in Taiwan, Hong Kong and Tibet.”</I>
